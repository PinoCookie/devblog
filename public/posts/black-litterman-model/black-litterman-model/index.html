Methodology

Black-Litterman

The main part of this project for me was understanding the Black-Litterman model itself.

$$ \Pi = \lambda\Sigma w_{mkt} $$

Where:

  • \(\Pi\) is the implied excess equilibrium return vector.
  • \(\lambda\) is the aversion coefficient.
  • \(\Sigma\) is the covariance matrix of excess returns
  • \(w_{mkt}\) is the market capitalization weight of the assests

This gives us a good starting point for the code.
